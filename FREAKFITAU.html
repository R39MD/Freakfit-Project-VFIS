About FreakFit

Based in Ho Chi Minh City, FreakFit is a passion project created by athletes for athletes. Our goal is to design sportswear that prioritizes performance, durability, and bold identity. Every product is built to move with you — whether you're running, lifting, or pushing your limits.

FreakFit isn't just clothing. It's a mindset built around strength, movement, and self-expression.
